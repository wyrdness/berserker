# Sources & References

## Primary Sources

### Old Norse Texts

- **Ynglinga saga** (c. 1225). Snorri Sturluson. Describes Odin's berserkers in detail.
- **Vatnsdœla saga** (13th century). Contains accounts of berserker warriors.
- **Egil's saga** (c. 1230). Features berserker characters and behavior.
- **Hrólfs saga kraka** (c. 14th century). Includes berserker warriors serving the king.
- **Grettis saga** (c. 14th century). Contains berserker encounters.

### Legal Texts

- **Grágás** (12th century). Medieval Icelandic law code that outlawed berserker behavior.
- **Gulathing Law** (11th-12th century). Norwegian law mentioning berserkers.
